Our Story

Built on Service.
Built to Last.

OEF Veterans Landscaping was founded by veterans who served in Operation Enduring Freedom, bringing the same commitment, discipline, and attention to detail from the battlefield to your backyard.

Based in Cape Coral, Florida, we proudly serve all of Southwest Florida with a full range of landscaping and property maintenance services. Whether it's a simple cleanup or a complete lot transformation, we treat every project like a mission — and we don't leave until the job is done right.

We offer special discounts for veterans and first responders because we know the sacrifice you've made. Licensed, insured, and recognized among the best in Florida — OEF Veterans is the team your property deserves.
